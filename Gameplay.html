A PvP gamemode where players can go against other player's cookie team. Challenge yourself and climb through the rankings to be the best in the server!

Guild Battle

Image of Guild Battle

Join guilds and meet new friends! Assist your guildmates by going against the different types of bosses. Climb the rankings with your guildmates to become the Number 1 guild in the server.

Gacha

Image of Gacha

Earn gems and spend them through gacha! Gacha allows you to meet new and even, strong cookies that can help boost your team power. The more cookies you have, the more combinations you can make to go against foes or players.

Toppings

Image of Toppings

Cookies aren't powerful from just levels itself, they would need toppings! Toppings are essential towards the power of your cookies, increase their power by levelling it up and making combinations with them.
